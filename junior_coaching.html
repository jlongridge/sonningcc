<h2>Winter and Summer Coaching</h2>

We run both indoor winter training for U9’s and above as well as summer coaching for all levels.  During Friday night’s Summer training sessions at King George's Field the Club bar will be open, and when the weather is set fair we will also light up the BBQ, so don't just drop them off but stay and sample the atmosphere of the club house!


<h3>Training sessions for our 2018 season will be as follows:</h3>

<h3>U7s</h3>

Summer Outdoor sessions – Blue Coat School, Sonning – Sunday 1st May to July 22nd inclusive, 10.30am to 12.00pm


<h3>U9s</h3>

Winter Indoor training – Blue Coat School, Sonning - Sunday 4 March to 25 March inclusive, plus 15th April and 22nd April, 12.30pm to 1.30pm


Summer Outdoor training – King George’s Field, Sonning – Friday 27th April to 20 July, 6.00pm to 7.15pm (6.00pm to 7.30pm from 1 June onwards)

<h3>U13s</h3>

<h3>U15s</h3>
